Our Machinery and Equipment

Excavator

Our state-of-the-art excavators are used for clearing sites and removing overburden.

Pay Loader

Our Payloaders are essential for mining operations, as they are used to load excavated materials onto haul trucks for transportation.

Grader

Graders are essential for levelling surfaces and preparing the ground for mining operations.

DRILLING RIG

Our drilling rigs are used to explore and sample mineral deposits in the licensed area.

TIPPER

Tipper trucks, also known as dump trucks, are used to transport loose materials such as ore, gravel, sand, and overburden.

LOW BED

Low bed trailers are designed to transport heavy mining equipment and machinery safely and efficiently.

TROMMEL

Trommels are crucial for the sorting and separation processes in mining. They are used to separate valuable minerals from waste rock and other materials.
